### GST/Tax Strategy Analysis
* **Tax Distribution**: The prices listed on the receipt are initially inclusive of GST (Post-Tax). However, a line-level discount labeled `Y 9999 GSTSAVING` is applied to each taxable item to subtract the GST amount, effectively converting the item prices to pre-tax (exclusive of GST). The subtotal is calculated using these pre-tax net amounts. Finally, GST @ 9.00% is calculated on the subtotal and added back to form the grand total. This strategy ensures that the final payment total is GST-inclusive, but the intermediate subtotal is pre-tax.
